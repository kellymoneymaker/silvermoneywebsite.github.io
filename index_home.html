Silvermoney

Kelly Moneymaker and Dan Silver unite for hard-hitting collision, Silvermoney. Their debut album, Goddess Killer, was Written, Performed, Engineered and Produced by the duo. The underlying concept of the album embodies the problems with how humans have treated Mother Earth. The Green Goddess is given a voice to embody her strength and vulnerability while showcasing the talents of Silver and Moneymaker. The release of Silvermoney's Goddess Killer spawned Ecohead Records, a label that embraces the idea of a greener, cleaner, more sustainable world by utilizing a solar-powered recording studio and eco-friendly manufacturing (soy-based inks, recycled plastic and cardboard packaging). New album scheduled for release in January 2018.

Dan Silver:

Music Creator and Producer in Los Angeles known as Silver Sessions specializes as a record producer, plays in a band, song writer, collaborator, Audio Engineer, Mixer, Remixer, Sonic Colorist, Sound Designer, Composer for Film, TV, Trailers, and Commercials, and performs several instruments. Dan is also V.P. of Creative for Riptide Music Group, Los Angeles and co-founder/talent curator of the Quantum Collective, an innovative music marketing collective that brings musicians, artists, celebrities and consumer brands together through original programs and live events.

Kelly Moneymaker

Alaskan-born Producer, Songwriter, Vocalist, Voiceover Actor, Music Supervisor/Editor and Multimedia Artist who tries her best to tread lightly upon sacred Mother Earth. She’s an avid supporter of National Resource Defense Council, Defenders of Wildlife, 350.org and The Earth Organization.
